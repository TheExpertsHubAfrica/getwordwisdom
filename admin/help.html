Getting Started

Welcome to GetWordWisdom Admin Dashboard!

Your central hub for managing blog posts and subscribers. This guide will help you navigate and use all the features effectively.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard has two main sections accessible from the sidebar:

  • Posts - Create, edit, and manage your blog content
  • Subscribers - View and manage your newsletter subscribers

Managing Posts

Creating a New Post

  1. Click "Create New Post"

    Located at the top of the Posts section. This opens the post editor.

  2. Enter Post Title

    Write a clear, compelling headline that captures your content.

    Example: "Finding Peace in God's Promises"
  3. Check the Slug

    Auto-generated URL-friendly version of your title. You can edit it if needed.

    Example: finding-peace-in-gods-promises
  4. Select Category

    Choose the most appropriate category:

    • Faith - Faith-building content
    • Devotionals - Daily spiritual nourishment
    • Christian Living - Practical life guidance
    • Teachings - Biblical teachings
    • Wisdom - Life wisdom and insights
  5. Enter Author Name

    Your name or the content creator's name (auto-filled but editable).

  6. Upload Featured Image (Optional)

    Click "Choose File" to upload an image that represents your post.

  7. Write Your Content

    Enter your blog post in the large text area. You can use HTML for formatting.

  8. Set Status
    • Draft - Save without publishing (not visible to readers)
    • Published - Make live on your website immediately
  9. Mark as Featured (Optional)

    Check this box to display the post prominently on your homepage.

  10. Save Your Post

    Click "Save Post" and wait for the success message.

Editing an Existing Post

  1. Find your post in the Posts table
  2. Click the Edit icon (pencil) in the Actions column
  3. Update any fields you need to change
  4. Click "Save Post" to apply changes

üí° Tip: Use Draft status while working on updates, then switch to Published when ready.

Understanding Your Subscribers

The Subscribers section shows everyone who has signed up for your newsletter.

Total Subscribers

All people who have ever subscribed

Active Subscribers

Currently subscribed (will receive emails)

Automatic Email Notifications

Your subscribers receive emails automatically in two situations:

Welcome Email

Sent immediately when someone subscribes

  • Warm welcome message
  • Platform introduction
  • Scripture encouragement

New Post Newsletter

Sent when you publish a new post

  • Post title and category
  • Content preview

Viewing Subscriber Information

The subscriber table displays:

  • Email - Subscriber's email address
  • Status - Active (receiving emails) or Unsubscribed
  • Date Subscribed - When they joined
  • Actions - Delete option

Removing Subscribers

Only delete subscribers if:

  • They requested removal
  • Email address is invalid or bouncing
  • It's a duplicate entry

üí° Tip: Let subscribers use the unsubscribe link in emails rather than deleting them manually.

Working with Images

Uploading Featured Images

Featured images make your posts more appealing and engaging.

  1. Click "Choose File"

    In the Featured Image section of the post editor

  2. Select Your Image

    Choose a relevant image from your computer

  3. Wait for Upload

    A progress bar shows the upload status

  4. Preview Displays

    You'll see a preview once upload is complete

Image Best Practices

‚úÖ

Do This

  • Use high-quality images
  • Minimum 1200px wide
  • JPG or PNG format
  • Relevant to content
  • Landscape orientation (16:9)

Avoid This

  • Blurry or pixelated images
  • Very large file sizes (>2MB)
  • Copyrighted images
  • Unrelated stock photos
  • Portrait orientation
